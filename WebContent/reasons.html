Employers are not required to keep OSHA injury and illness records if the company had

  • 10 or fewer employees at all times during the last calendar year
  • and/or for any establishment classified in the SIC codes below, unless they are asked in writing to do so by OSHA, the Bureau of Labor Statistics (BLS), or a state agency operating under the authority of OSHA or the BLS.

All employers, including those partially exempted by reason of company size or industry classification, must report to OSHA any workplace incident that results in a fatality or the hospitalization of three or more employees.

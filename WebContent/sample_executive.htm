Pacific Industrial Contractor Screening (PICS) was established to assist companies with a thorough audit program for all contractors working at their facilities. PICS sets the highest standards possible in regard to contractor qualifications as they relate to federally or state-mandated regulations and/or specific operator requirements.

Our services are designed to standardize and centralize the contractor qualification process, lessen the burden on contractors, as well as minimize potential incidents for the contractors and operators. PICS not only reviews a detailed audit questionnaire, but we provide onsite auditing of all contractors to verify any pertinent documentation that solidifies what has been filled out in the questionnaire.

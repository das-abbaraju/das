Oil Sands Land Reclamation Training: Review Test (5 questions)
1)
What is the current disturbed land reclamation percentage?
 



Currently, Suncor reclaims 7% of land used for oil sands.
2)
What is one of the biggest challenges Suncorp faces as an energy industry leader?
 



One of Suncor's primary priorities is reducing the environmental impact of its overall business and production.
3)
What is the target year for 100% reclamation of land use?
 



With the full scale advent of polymer technologies, Suncor has targeted 2015 for 100% land use reclamation.
4)
What is the effect of adding the polymer solution to suspension of fine tailings?
 



The polymer solution added to fine tailing suspension separtes water from clay in a very short time.
5)
What is Suncor's goal for the dried clay fields that result from tailing pond reclamation?
 



Each pond will be reclaimed as a dry clay field for revegetation.
